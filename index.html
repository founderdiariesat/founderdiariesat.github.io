Mr. Dhruvam Thaker, Founder of The Smart Taxi

Failing in 12th board exams can be disheartening but not giving up in spite of that takes core values like resilience and grit. Hence saying that here Founder Dairies have Mr. Dhruvam Thaker the founder of The SMART Taxi, a car rental company that spreads across 30 cities in India. Mr. Dhruvam Thaker hailing from Ahmedabad academically brilliant student, unfortunately, faced failure in board exams. Despite this, he ended pursuing a Diploma in mechanical engineering with a silver medal.

Take Away 1

One book can change your life but to reach that one book, you need patience and go through many. Similarly, one customer can change life waiting for that can be of 90 days at times.

Take Away 2

Always remember the purpose of what you started your business at all stages.

Take Away 3

Instead of hunting for the price, stay intact to transparency and integrity with customers without compromising quality.

Mr. Rajit Bhattacharya, CEO, Data Sutram

Data Sutram - Next GOOGLE for the business. Data Sutram evolved from the hackathon conducted by the Retail brand, where the team ended up as Winners. Geospatial data and demographics can enrich your business decision. Here comes Data Sutram who is an AI-driven location intelligence startup. Rajit Bhattacharya who at a very young age had an inclination to take risks to start their own venture is one of the founders of Data Sutram. He has a very interesting and profound personality.

Take Away 1

Startup is not a habit you want to inculcate or start. Startups are born out of problems faced which need some quintessential solution.

Take Away 2

Do not get intimidated by the hype of Data Science and evolving Artificial intelligence. Instead, focus on the basics, understand mathematics and statistics.

Take Away 3

Patience is a parameter that needs due consideration in times of decision-making. Impatience can lead to Bad decisions.

Ph.D. Pankaj Gupta, Co-Founder, and CTO at DRIMCo GmbH

Where there is a will there's a way this quote fits for Dr.Pankaj Gupta with a Ph.D. from TU MUNICH who is also the founder of DrimCo Gmbh. Dr.Pankaj Gupta with his will traveled a huge journey from a small village in Madhya Pradesh to Munich. Once working in a small garlic factory as a labor founded a company in Munich related to Natural language processing. His in-depth knowledge and the splendid journey will definitely inspire you.

Take Away 1

Embracing new technology is not just enough, understand the technology value it carries and its application is very important.

Take Away 2

Believe in taking a calculative risk.

Take Away 3

Always thrive for innovation, an idea is worthless if it has no real application.
